Places of Interest in Kodaikanal

Silver Cascade Falls

Silver Cascade is one of Kodaikanal’s most popular attractions. Here, watch shimmering water flowing down a 180-foot-tall (55-meter) cliff and monkeys playing mischievously on slippery rocks. Silver Cascade is the result of excess water that flows from Kodaikanal Lake. It takes its name from the silver-colored appearance of the water, which glistens in the bright sunshine.

Pillar Rocks

The three gigantic Pillar-Shaped Rocks in Kodaikanal attract 95% of the tourists who visits Kodaikanal. Chambers between the 2 Shoulder to Shoulder Pillars are best known as Devils Kitchen(Now Guna Caves).

Pine Forrest

Pine Forests is one of the most popular places to see in Kodaikanal. A preserved heritage of the Kodaikanal, it presents mesmerizing nature that has been preserved very carefully.The sight of umpteen numbers of pine trees, standing majestically, welcoming you their world is nothing less than a nature’s wonder.
